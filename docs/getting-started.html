Create an Account

PRP uses a secure email + password sign-up backed by Supabase Auth. Your data is private and protected with row-level security from day one.

  1. Go to web.prp-app.website or open the Windows app.
  2. Tap Sign Up on the welcome screen.
  3. Enter your email address and choose a strong password.
  4. Check your inbox for a verification email and confirm your address.
  5. You'll be redirected to your dashboard — you're in.
Already have an account? Tap Log In on the welcome screen and enter your credentials.

Your First Login

On first login, PRP greets you with a personalized greeting and a live clock. The home dashboard shows all 4 resource modules at a glance. Take a moment to familiarise yourself with the sidebar navigation:

  • Time — Calendar, schedule, tasks
  • Finance — Accounts, net worth, transactions
  • Energy — Focus timer, goals, ideas
  • Health — Habits, fasting, daily metrics

Set Up Your Profile

Head to Profile → Settings to personalise PRP:

  1. Display name — Shown in the personalized greeting header.
  2. Avatar — Upload a photo. Stored securely in Supabase Storage.
  3. Theme — Choose dark, light, or follow system preference.
  4. Language — English (LTR) or Arabic (RTL) supported.
