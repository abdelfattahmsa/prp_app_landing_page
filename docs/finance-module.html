Finance Module

Finance Module Guide

Get a complete picture of your financial life — bank accounts, investments, debt, expenses, and net worth, all in one place with live multi-currency support.

On this page

Overview Adding Bank Accounts Tracking Transactions Investments & Stocks Debt Management Net Worth Dashboard Multi-Currency & FX Rates Analytics & Charts

Overview

The Finance module gives you a real-time snapshot of your entire financial life. It aggregates balances from all your accounts — bank, investment, and debt — into a single net worth figure, updated live as you log transactions.

Adding Bank Accounts

  1. Go to Finance → Accounts → Add Account.
  2. Choose account type: Bank Account, Investment, Credit Card, or Loan.
  3. Search for your bank — PRP includes autocomplete for 35+ Egyptian banks with branch data pre-filled.
  4. Enter the account name, currency, and current balance.
  5. Tap Save. The account balance is immediately reflected in your net worth.
Tip: Add all your accounts — including debt like credit cards and loans — to get an accurate net worth figure. PRP subtracts liabilities automatically.

Tracking Transactions

Every income or expense you log updates your account balance in real time:

  1. Go to Finance → Transactions → Add.
  2. Select Income or Expense.
  3. Enter amount, category (e.g. Food, Rent, Salary), date, and optional notes.
  4. Choose which account to debit or credit.
  5. Tap Save — the spending-by-category chart updates instantly.

Investments & Stocks

PRP connects to the Alpha Vantage API to fetch live stock prices for your holdings:

  1. Add an Investment account and set its type to Stocks or Portfolio.
  2. Enter each holding: ticker symbol, number of shares, and purchase price.
  3. PRP fetches the current market price and calculates your unrealized gain/loss.
  4. Total portfolio value flows into your net worth automatically.
Stock price updates depend on your Alpha Vantage API key. Free tier allows up to 25 requests/day.

Debt Management

Add loans, credit cards, and any other liabilities as Debt accounts. PRP tracks:

  • Outstanding balance updated each time you log a payment.
  • Debt-to-asset ratio shown on the net worth dashboard.
  • Monthly repayment schedule if you enter the minimum payment amount.

Net Worth Dashboard

The net worth dashboard is the heart of the Finance module. It shows:

  • Total assets — sum of all bank, investment, and cash accounts.
  • Total liabilities — sum of all debt accounts.
  • Net worth — assets minus liabilities, with a trend line over time.
  • Cashflow chart — monthly income vs. expenses bar chart.

Multi-Currency & FX Rates

PRP fetches live foreign exchange rates so you can hold accounts in different currencies and see everything converted to your base currency:

  1. Go to Profile → Settings → Base Currency and choose your preferred currency (e.g. EGP, USD, EUR).
  2. Each account can be set to a different currency — PRP converts at the live rate.
  3. FX rates refresh automatically when you open the Finance module.

Analytics & Charts

Finance analytics are available under Finance → Reports:

  • Spending by category — donut chart of where your money goes each month.
  • Cashflow — income vs. expenses over the last 3, 6, or 12 months.
  • Net worth trend — line chart of your net worth growing over time.
